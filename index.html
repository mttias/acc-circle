The Hammertime Pledge

The Hammertime Pledge is an experimental program to keep you on track to meet your rationality goals. You join by taking a pledge to start and follow-through the Hammertime program for 30 days. Each day, you will be sent a reminder and and a link to check your progress. Are you feeling accountable?
